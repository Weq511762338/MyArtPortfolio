Rise to Fall: 3D Accordian Book

Home / Portfolio
Date of Creation: Jun 13, 2023

Title: Rise to Fall - Accordian Book

Category: 3D Craftmaking

Materials: Pen, Acrylic

This is a class project exploring line art, tension, and patterns in a 3D persepctive. I employed black and white contrast and carved out the paper to create lens with different shapes. Seeing through, the color in the back can serve as color on the carved pattern.
