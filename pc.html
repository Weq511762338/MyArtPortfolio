Drawing & Illustration: Colonized

Home / Portfolio
Date of Creation: Dec 3, 2021

Title: Colonized

Category: Drawing & Illustration

Materials: Color Pencil

Award: Silver Key in Scholastic Art 2022

The artwork, "Colonized", as its name suggests, depicts a human being overtaken and 'colonized' by a computer. Becoming a computer with the exposed electronics, the human seems to be completely controlled. With explicit details and colorful illustrations, this work symbolizes the potential concern of computers and networks replacing and manipulating humans one day in the future. At the same time, it demonstrates the fact that internet, computer, and technology have became more involved our life, bringing benefits and convenience on every aspect of society. What this art piece conveys is that we shall be aware of how much technology is rooted in our life while embracing our personality and lifestyles.
