Drawing & Illustration: Colonized

Home / Portfolio
Date of Creation: Dec 3, 2021

Title: Colonized

Materials: Color Pencil

Award: Silver Key in Scholastic Art 2022

The work, Colonized, as its name suggested, illustrates a human being occupied and "colonized" by a computer. Becoming a computer with the electronics exposed open, the human seems to be completely controlled. With explicit details and colorful illustration, this work symbolizes the potential concern of computer and network replacing and manipulating humans on day in the future.
