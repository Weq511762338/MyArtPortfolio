Date of Creation: Year 2021-2023

Title: The Day on the Ocean

This work employs complement and contrast of color. The variety of the blue and the mix of warm color in the background create a moody emotion in general on the ocean. The dark brown color of the boat illustrates solemnity and solitude, as well as the unstoppable motion of powering forward.

Title: Oranges

This work simply has a plateful of oranges. With the relationship in their positions such as blocking and shadowing, they are depicted more vivid and realistic. The contrast of the light and shadows further creates the spacial sense on the canvas.

Title: Limes

Similar to the previous work, this art focuses on showing illustrating the shadowing and texture of the objects. With a relatively vibrant green color, the dim and cold color background creates a contrast to emahpsize the main objects in the front.

Title: Summer

This art depicts the lemons and a few ice cubes on the page. Illustrating the unique transparency of the ice and sharp-edged lemons, this work expresses a sense of freshness and coolness. The combination of color blue and yellow and pure white makes further conveys such emotions.
